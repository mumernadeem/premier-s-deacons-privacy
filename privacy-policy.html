Privacy Policy for Deacons Supermarket Ltd

Last Updated: 27-06-2025

1. Introduction

Deacons Supermarket Ltd (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the Premier’s Deacons mobile application (“App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with UK data protection laws, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Age-Restricted Products Notice

By accepting this policy, you acknowledge that:

  • Smoking and vaping (including e-cigarettes and vapes) pose serious health risks and are a leading cause of preventable death and disease.
  • Deacons Supermarket Ltd sells nicotine products and vapes exclusively to adults of legal age (18+ in the UK).
  • Deacons Supermarket Ltd sells alcohol exclusively to adults of legal drinking age (18+ in the UK).
  • ID verification may be required upon delivery or pickup for age-restricted products.
  • We reserve the right to refuse sales if age cannot be verified.

3. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information You Provide:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery/billing address
  • Payment details (processed securely via third-party payment processor)
  • Account login credentials
  • Age verification data (for restricted products)

b) Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device information (IP address, device type, OS)
  • App usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Location data (if enabled for delivery/store locator)

c) Cookies & Similar Technologies:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and improve our services.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfil orders (including age-restricted items)
  • Verify age eligibility for restricted products
  • Communicate order confirmations and updates
  • Improve our App and services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Contractual necessity (order fulfilment)
  • Legal obligation (age verification)
  • Consent (where required)
  • Legitimate interests (service improvement)

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share data with:

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law (e.g., age-verification records).
